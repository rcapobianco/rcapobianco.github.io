## About me

My name is Rogério Capobianco, and I have a PhD in physics by the University of São Paulo. I work in theoretical physics, with interests in gravity, dynamical systems, integrability, and mathematical modeling.

This page is part of the project **Phase Space Echoes**, where I discuss simple and fundamental problems in physics, sometimes from a didactic perspective, sometimes from a more technical or research-oriented point of view.
