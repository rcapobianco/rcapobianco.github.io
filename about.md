## About me

My name is Rogério Capobianco, and I hold a PhD in Physics from the University of São Paulo. I work in theoretical physics, with interests in gravity, dynamical systems, integrability, and mathematical modeling.

This page is part of the project **Phase Space Echoes**, where I discuss simple and fundamental problems in physics, sometimes from a didactic perspective, and sometimes from a more technical or research-oriented point of view.

My main research interests include general relativity, modified gravity, nonlinear dynamics, and numerical methods applied to physical systems.
