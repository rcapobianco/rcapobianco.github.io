Hello, and welcome to my page.

This is a space to share some ideas and discuss physics at different levels, always keeping a didactic perspective. Some of the discussions here may be research-oriented -- for example, a detailed discussion of a paper or of a specific model -- while others may come from long-standing curiosity about physical systems. The goal is to explore the systems, their theoretical foundations, and the structure of their solutions, whether exact or numerical, and sometimes to compare different methods or approaches. 

This is my personal page to discuss ideas that do not always fit well into scientific articles. I will use accessible language and keep the presentation as simple as possible, while remaining physically and mathematically accurate and serious. The topics may range from classical mechanics and dynamical systems to general relativity, gravity, and cosmology. 

However, the content here is not peer-reviewed, and the discussions are in constant revision and improvement. Feel free to take the material here as a motivation or a supporting material for your studies. If you built something based on these notes, I kindly ask for proper acknowledgement. 

If you have questions, comments, suggestions, or constructive criticism, you can contact me at rogerio.capobianco@gmail.com.
